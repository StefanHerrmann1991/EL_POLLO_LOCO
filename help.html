Pepe is the protagonist of this game. You can control him with the following controls.
Jump – Space or A Button
Throw – Arrow Up or W
Tip: Throw bottles, they are mighty weapons against the chickens. Some chickens are only killable with these objects.
Dodge – Arrow Down or S
Tip: Dodge can be useful to avoid some attacks of stronger chickens.
Left – Arrow Left or A
Right – Arrow Right or D
Full-screen – F11 works fine on desktop, but not on all responsive devices.
Coins will bring you more life even over 100 percent, that you don't die so fast.
